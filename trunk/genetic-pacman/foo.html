CSSE 413: Machine Learning Programming Assignment

Overview

The purpose of this assignment is to explore a machine learning technique of your choice in more detail. You will use a modified version of the Pacman Tournament player to implement and experiment with a particular technique. The experiments you conduct are as important as the qualiy of your implementation.

Specifics

  • Based on the feedback, I received, I assigned the following teams and topics
  • For this project, you need to perform some research. You need to
    • Study the technique beyond what we introduce in class.
    • Either develop or possibly download and extend existing Python code that implements those techniques and adapt them to your needs.
    • Develop a good way of representing the domain to make learning possible.
    • Experiment with several layouts (see the layouts folder) and types of game play (see below.)
  • A large portion of your grade will depend on the experiments you conduct as well as a careful analysis of those experiments. Your grade for this programming assignment will not depend on whether your chosen approach works well or not, although you need to do a good job implementing it.
  • We are going to make a few modifications to the code. In particular, we will have:
    • Complete vision of food, pacmen and ghosts
    • Eating pacmen gets points (to be specific, 10 points)
  • You might be interested in the following options:
    • python capture.py --record will record a game. Play it back with --replay=
    • python capture.py -x NUMTRAINING will specify number of training games to play
    • python capture.py --time=TIME will specify the length of a game in number of moves
    • python capture.py -f will fix the random seed
    • Each of these options are available from python capture.py -h

Administrative Details

  • In addition to developing software and conducting experiments, you are asked to write up a report containing the results of your experiments and a careful analysis of the strengths and weaknesses of your approach. This report should contain some tables or figures summarizing the results of your experiments.
  • The report and software is tentatively due on Monday, October 22nd at midnight.
  • You will give a brief (5 minute) presentation of your results and evaluation during class on the day following the due date.
